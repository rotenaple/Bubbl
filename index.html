Bubbl

a font by rotenaple
Bubbl is a display font with thick and rounded characters. It is designed for short-form and large-format applications, such as billboards, posters, logos, headlines, and book covers. Bubbl is a fun and playful font that catches the eye and stands out. Bubbl supports standard English characters, as well as IPA characters and Greek letters.
